## What this site collects

Nothing. There are no user accounts, no comments, no contact forms, no analytics scripts, and no third-party trackers on this site.

The only data the site stores about you is what your browser sends to the hosting platform (Vercel) for routine request logging — your IP address and user-agent string for a short retention window, used to detect abuse. This is standard for any website and is not shared.

## What this site stores

Headlines, links, and short summaries from public RSS feeds of health authorities and major news outlets. These are cached in a database so the site loads quickly without re-fetching every feed on every visit. Articles include the source name, publication date, headline, summary excerpt, and a link to the original article on the source's website.

No article content is republished beyond what RSS feeds expose. Links go directly to the original source.

## Cookies and local storage

The site uses `localStorage` only to remember your theme preference (light or dark). This stays on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

No cookies are set by this site. The browser may receive cookies from Vercel and from CARTO map tiles for routine network operations, governed by their privacy policies.

## Third-party services

This site is hosted on Vercel and pulls map tiles from CARTO. RSS feed contents come from the original publishers (WHO, CDC, ECDC, PAHO, BBC, Reuters, and others). Article links you click navigate away from this site to those publishers' websites, which set their own cookies and tracking.

## Contact

To request a correction or report an issue, use the contact link in the footer. This site has no other communication channel.
